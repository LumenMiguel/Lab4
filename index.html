Luzon

Luzon is considered as the most important island in the Philippines
not just because there are many tourist spots in Luzon. When we talk
about history, the most tragic and revolutionary events that made what
the Philippines is today happened in Luzon where great Heroes were
born like Dr. Jose Rizal (Filipino National Hero).

If you happen to visit Luzon or you want to go there for a vacation
or any reason and you want to experience some tourist spots in Luzon
, the island have a lot to offer. Whether you want to go to historical
centers, mountains, falls, parks, caves and rivers, surely you will
enjoy them.

Visayas

Visayas is a very calm region surrounded with beautiful beaches,
caves and mountains. It is a perfect destination for relaxation and
is very known as the “Islands of Travelers” in the Philippines.

If you are looking for a destination in the Philippines to relax,
enjoy or party rock during night perhaps you can pin Visayas in your
list and visit amazing tourist spots in Visayas.

Mindanao

Mindanao is the second largest island group in the Philippines and
it is also called as the Land of Promise. More of the Filipinos here are
in agricultural industry since the land has a lot to offer. It has the
largest group of minorities in the Philippines.

Mindanao is a home of many species since it is mostly covered in
forest and jungle. It has many mountains, caves, lakes, falls and rivers.
It is where you can find the highest mountain in the Philippines which is
Mount Apo. Philippines’ tallest waterfall, Limunsudan Falls in Iligan City.
